Overview

Sit, then reset.

The Storage Bench keeps sitting and sorting in one place so children can pause without leaving the clean-up zone.

Use

Visible storage works faster.

Open cubbies keep the daily logic simple, making the bench more about access than concealment.

Placement

Fits the room edge.

It tends to work best along a wall, where the top can also hold a tray, lamp, or small display object.

Catalogue note

The bench is treated as a workhorse piece in the catalogue, meant to absorb repetition without making the room feel purely utilitarian.
