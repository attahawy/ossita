Overview

Storage that follows play.

The Toy Trolley is planned for rooms where activity shifts through the day and fixed storage is not always enough.

Use

Open by default.

It stays easy to load and unload quickly, which matters more than hiding contents in high-frequency reset zones.

Placement

Useful at the edge of a scene.

It can park beside a play table, bench, or reading area and move when the room needs to open up again.

Catalogue note

The trolley is intended as a flexible utility piece within the catalogue, suited to families that reset one room several times in a day.
