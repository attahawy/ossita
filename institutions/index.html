How we work

A straightforward process from brief to install.

Tell us the site type, age range, quantity, and finish direction, and we will help shape a practical proposal.

1. Brief

Share the project details.

Send dimensions, room photos, target quantities, and any brand or finish preferences you want reflected in the furniture.

2. Design

Confirm the layout and range.

We review proportions, circulation, access, and storage needs so the furniture supports the room instead of crowding it.

3. Produce

Manufacture to order.

Pieces are built in the quantities and finish set agreed for your school, nursery, or preschool project.

4. Deliver

Install and hand over.

We can support delivery, assembly, and setup so the space opens cleanly and stays easy to maintain.
